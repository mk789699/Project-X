John Keats Junior school is a co-Educational Junior School affiliated to government of Bihar for educating students from class 1st to 8th. The school has been operating officially under the yugeshwar singh memorial education and human welfare Trust. School also prepares students for competitive exams like Navodaya Vidyalaya class 6th entrance exam under direction of qualified trained teacher. John Keats Junior school established on 6th January,1996 in response to the continuous demand of rural people of area.The school is situated beside State highway-77 in Balha-Madhsudanpur ,Pupari,Sitamarhi from VIA-Janakpur road.A team of highly qualified and trained faculties are determined to nourish curious minds. Students are also provided with high class hostel facilities for their all arround development.School aims to enhance physical,mental and spiritual growth of child through a perfect amalgamation of academics,sports,co-circular activities.

A journey was started on 6th January,1996 with just one target to make a lower class family achieve a higher class dream through their children . Every illiterate father want his child to much literate but economic barrier or society barrier act as a mountain for such desires we have eradicated such trends. We since 1996 been guilding the students towards achieving the very first or elementary target that is for getting Admission in JNV. And the best part is that,from one student in 1996,till now more than 400 students have been selected and they are serving in various fields. Now , regular classroom programme for class 1 to 8 has also started since 2002,and In 2014 , our institution is fully certified as well as got affliation from Government of Bihar. We shall always be committed to boost our result to a great level as more and more students are becoming part of institution. Our institution has brought an educational reform in the society and shall ever remain a milestone.

John Keats Junior School is providing excellent educational facility and opportunities that are highly necessary for students.We aim to shape future of our students in such a manner that they can face and overcome upcoming challenges in life. Our vision is based on these following values:- 1.Focus:- Students are being focused to reach their diverse goals in smart manner. 2.Excellence:-Maintain a high standard of integrity and performance leading to the achivement of academics. 3.Life long Learning:-Being as enthusiastic and independent thinkers that leads for their growth. 4.Diversity:-Diversity gives a challenge to students by fostering a competitive envirnonment among students for JNV preparation.
